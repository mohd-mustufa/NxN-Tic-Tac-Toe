NxN Tic Tac Toe
Game Rules

Objective: The goal of the game is to be the first to form a line of your symbol (either 'X' or 'O) of a certain length, depending on the board size.

Board Size:You can choose the board size. The standard size is 3x3, but you can select a larger size, such as 4x4 or 5x5.

Players: Two players take turns. One is 'X,' and the other is 'O.'

Turns: 'X' goes first, and then players take turns to place their symbol on the board.

Winning Condition:
- 3x3 board: On a 3x3 board, you need 3 of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal to win.
- Larger boards: (n x n, where n is the board size), you need (n-1) consecutive symbols in a row, column, or diagonal to win.

Draw: If the board is filled, and no player has won, the game is a draw (tie).

Restart: You can start a new game or change the board size at any time.

Enjoy the Game: Have fun and enjoy the challenge of winning!
